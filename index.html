Rules and original behavior

Each player privately opens this page on their phone, rolls their own dice, and keeps the screen hidden.

The first player announces a bid: a minimum quantity of one face across all players, such as "seven 3s". Ones are wild and count as the predicted number.

On your turn, raise the bid or challenge it. A raise is either a higher quantity of the same face, or any quantity of a higher face. On a challenge, everyone reveals their screens. If at least the bid quantity exists, counting wild ones, the bidder wins; otherwise the challenger wins.

The loser loses one die and opens the next round. At zero dice, that player is out.

Variant: after a non-one bid, you may bid 1s only by halving the quantity before increasing it. After a 1s bid, return to another face only by doubling the quantity before increasing it.
